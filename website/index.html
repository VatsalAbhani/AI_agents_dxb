    05 / QUESTIONS

    Asked, answered.

    Isn’t this just a chatbot?+

    A chatbot talks to your customers unsupervised. This is an AI agent inside a control system: a policy gate that blocks risky wording before it exists on a customer’s phone, a human approval step for everything outbound, and a cryptographic record of all of it.

    What happens when the AI writes something wrong?+

    That’s the point of the Guard. The draft is blocked before sending, a compliant rewrite is requested, and your manager still has the final say. The blocked draft is recorded too — proof the control fired.

    Which channels does it cover?+

    WhatsApp-first — where Dubai leads actually live — plus website forms and email. During the pilot the approved message can be sent by your team with one tap while we connect your WhatsApp Business API.

    What does “tamper-evident” actually mean?+

    Every recorded action carries a fingerprint of the one before it. Edit any entry — even a comma — and every fingerprint after it stops matching. Anyone can re-run the check without trusting us, our servers, or you.

    We already answer fast.+

    At 3am? Every lead, every time, in the same compliant tone, with a record you can audit? Fast is a shift schedule. This is a system.
